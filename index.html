Learn design and technical skills

WHAT WE DO

Learn design and technical skills in a residential environment.

Even if you can't afford college, or you're in a transition period in life, we will give you a good environment for learning.

We look for people 18-24 who are bright, ambitious, and hard-working.

We expect no prior experience, and we welcome people from any background.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST

We charge no tuition.

For two months before the start of our program, we will help you polish up your resume try to match you up with a job near where we will be. There's a good chance you'll be able to work a part-time job.

If you are matched to a job, the costs of this program will be covered. Otherwise, you can choose to not commit to the program. If you would like to pay by other means, living costs generally run $1000 a month. However, we charge only $500 a month (for rent). The program runs 3 months, though you may elect to stay for a second cohort. The rest is up to you, though you should have enough money to feed yourself.

WHAT IS THE PROGRAM LIKE

You get to live together with the brightest people from around the country for 3 months.

We have fun outings, but really, you decide what to do and everyone has a say in how your experience turns out.

Because we are in the Bay Area, you will be close to mentorship from some of the best in the industry.
